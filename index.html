Projects

Clique Book

Clique Book is an app that helps you as a club president manage your club's members. Create your club's handbook, manage member lists, and track which members have viewed and agreed to the terms of your handbook. This was built during a month-long sprint with a 5-person team.

LifeGPA

LifeGPA is a habit tracking app that rolls up your successes into one simple number to make it easy to see how well you're doing. Add habits that you'd like to incorporate into your life. Then log in each day and check them off. I originally built the backend during a 4-day sprint on a 5-person team, but have since rebuilt it from the ground up myself.

GitHub | Site

InMate Skills

InMake Skills helps match employers with prison labor. Prison administators can register their prison and manage their labor pool. Employers can search for labor that has the skills that they need. This was built during a 4 day sprint.
